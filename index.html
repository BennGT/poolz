Safety

Quick reminders before dosing
Do not mix chemicals Add products separately and keep them in their own containers.
Acid into water Add acid carefully to pool water. Never add water into acid.
Space additions Run the pump and allow mixing time before adding another chemical.
Retest first Retest before redosing, especially after acid, chlorine or large corrections.
Indoor CYA Indoor pools often avoid cyanuric acid. Follow local rules and product labels.
