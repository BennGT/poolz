Safety

Disclaimer: Recommendations are provided as a general guide only. Always follow product label instructions and seek advice from a qualified pool or water treatment professional when needed.
Indoor pools often avoid cyanuric acid. Follow local rules and product labels.
Add products separately and keep them in their own containers.
Add acid carefully to pool water. Never add water into acid.
Run the pump and allow mixing time before adding another chemical.
Retest before redosing, especially after acid, chlorine or large corrections.
